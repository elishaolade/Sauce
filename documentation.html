SAUCE

Thank you for downloading the Sauce theme. This documentation covers everything that you need to know to hit the ground running. If you do not find an answer to your question, feel free to contact us at devtechla@gmail.com

Uploading/Installing

  1. Download theme package from themeforest.net.
  2. Unzip downloaded file. Unzipped folder contains theme file and documentation folder.
  3. Go to admin page of your Shopify store, click Upload theme button.
  4. Open the folder unzipped in above step and find Venedor.zip. Upload theme file and publish it

Theme Settings

Logo

Upload your company logo. PNG file format is recommended.

Company Name

Enter your company name. Alternative to logo.

Newsletter

Choose to have the newspaper option.

Payment Options

Select your payment options for your store. (i.e Paypal, Visa, Mastercard etc.)

Checkout
  • Background Image for Banner
  • Custom Image for Logo
  • Background Area for Main Content Area
  • Background Area for Order Summary
  • Typography for Headings and Body
  • Colors for Accents, Buttons, Errors

Section Settings

Hero Section

Hero section is a carousel that can display images that you upload to your website. At the moment, it takes three different sizes for desktop and mobile devices.

Adding slides

  1. Click "Add Slide"
  2. Enter the heading of the slide. Hint: This is your hook that makes visitors want to learn more.
  3. Enter the content of the slide. Hint: This is where your details of your slide go.
  4. Choose the positioning. Do you want your written content on your slide on the right or left.
  5. Select your images for the different screen sizes.
  6. Select your the collection that is associated with your slide.
Featured Collection Section

Display a collection that you want to feature on the home page of your website.

  1. Add featured collection
  2. Selection collection to display
Footer
  1. Add featured collection
  2. Select menu to display. Hint: Limit of 2.
